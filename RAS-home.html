RAS is an ongoing roleplay run by Amina, featuring the characters Stripey, Supah, Nee, and Choko from PawClub. During RAS, they are all members of a welcoming committee at their high school, RAS, to watch over a new group of foreign exchange students, who are the members of the rp. RAS has some gaming aspects, which are documented in this site.
