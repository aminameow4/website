Don't Take it Seriously is a partially slice of life, partially comedic comic thought up and drawn by Amina , and read over by Christine. It's about Bluzie Retsbew, a teenager in the fictional country of Anya'xe, who has a horrible tendency to say the wrong things at the wrong time, and her learning to become more mature and grown up.
