Human
Homo sapiens

Home Planet: Earth
Brief Synopsis: Humans are unable to naturally perform magic. They’re the “default” species. They appear the most ‘normal’ and ‘natural’ to us.
Native Region: Earth

Language(s) Spoken: A lot. Humans in the main Felzhverse species all speak English at least, though.

Key Physical Attributes: Generic base for creatures that qualify as “humans”.

Anatomy: Basic primate with two arms and legs. No tails, pointed ears, etc. Average height for female is approx. 5’ 4”, and males are approx. 5’ 9”.

Common Behaviors: Inquisitive.

Eating Habits: They're omnivores.

Preferred Habitat: Any society, they are very social creatures that need to maintain contact with other living things to stay alive and healthy.

Family Structure: Two parents, and usually offspring.

Life Stages:
  • Baby:
    • One year and younger
  • Toddler:
    • Two to 3 years
  • Young child:
    • 4 to 9 years
  • Older child:
    • 10 to 13 years
  • Adolescent:
    • 14 to 20 years
  • Young adult:
    • 21 to 30 years
  • Adult:
    • 31 to 65 years
  • Elder:
    • 65+ years
Way of Dress: Human Clothes™

Religion?: Humans have many different religions. The most popular globally are Christianity, Islam, and Hinduism.

Characters belonging to species: Nathan,Hypatia, Nadzieja, Jael, Nikita
