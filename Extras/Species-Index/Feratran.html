Feratran
fera commūtāta

Home Planet: Earth
Brief Synopsis: Feratrans are humans that are cursed. They’re big fluffy buff dudes.
Native Region: Europe

Language(s) Spoken: Many, speak English in story.

Key Physical Attributes: Tall

Anatomy: Feratrans have four different types:
  • Fera Form- includes chest fur/feathers, pubic fur/feathers, thin fur/feathers all around their body.
  • Human Form- may include traits from their Fera Form (Ex. Bubs’ eyes).
  • Anthro Form- They’re furries. Just furries.
  • Animal Form- They can take form of any non-extinct animals.

Common Behaviors: Animalistic behaviors (though most can be controlled)

Eating Habits: Feratrans can be Omnivores, Carnivores, or Herbivores.

Preferred Habitat: Depends on the Feratran. Some live in forests and live as animals. Most prefer to live among humans.

Family Structure: Feratrans adopted human values of family. Though basic instincts hold.

Life Stages: Feratran animal forms age base of the equivalent of human to animals years. (EX: Bubs is still a kitten at age 16, she’s not full grown and would be about 8 to 9 months in cat years)

Way of Dress: Feratrans wear human style though, clothes are enchanted so they disappear, grow, or shrink when they change forms.

Religion?: They have “Gods” kinda. They were the first Feratrans to renter society.

Characters belonging to species: Bubs, Monkey, and Reizo
