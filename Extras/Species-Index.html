Eyote
homo notātus
(plural:Eyote.)

Brief Synopsis: A species similar to humans (homo sapiens), but they appear taller and have different melanin content in their skin, as well as completely different dietary restrictions.
Come From: Anya'xe Language(s) Spoken: Many, but Eyote is the most common language in Anya’xe.
Key Physical Attributes: They generally appear the same as humans, although Eyote tend to be approximately ~5 inches taller on average. Aside from height, their most notable difference from humans would be the markings across their faces and bodies. The markings vary from being lines to dots based off the ethnic group they belong to.
Anatomy: Description
Common Behaviors: Description
Eating Habits: Eyote are primarily carnivores, though if meat is hard to find some Jo are able to temporarily regress to eating primarily plants. It’s not healthy for them to have largely plant-based diets though.
Preferred Habitat:

  • Gan:Tend to live in cities and highly cluttered places, as it’s easier to blend in.
  • Jo: Love to be in open spaces to run around.

Mating Rituals: Description
Family Structure: Families are very similar to those of humans, although they are more matriarchal. Children are expected to get married and have children of their own soon after secondary school. Couples without children are seen as oddities.
Life Stages:
  • Baby: Birth- One year
  • Toddler: 2-4
  • Young child: 5- around 8
  • Older child: ~8- ~10
  • Adolescent: 10-15
  • Young adult: ~15-22

Way of Dress: If they wear anything at all, they wear flowy clothing. Eyote prefer being comfortable and casual in all aspects of life, so they don’t really like being bogged down by their clothing affecting movement. Tight clothes are seen as rebellious and promiscuous. Why should you need to see the outline of someone’s body? In recent years, influence by western human societies has started to play a role in fashion choices of adolescent Eyote.
Religion?: Eyote worship the 13 gods of their calendar.
Characters belonging to species: Bluzie, Random, Lily, Clover



Feratran
fera commūtāta
(plural:[thing].)

Brief Synopsis: Feratrans are humans that are cursed. They’re big fluffy buff dudes.
Land in Story: Description
Land of Origin: Description
Language(s) Spoken: Description
Key Physical Attributes: Tall
Anatomy: Feratrans have four different types:

  • Fera Form- includes chest fur/feathers, pubic fur/feathers, thin fur/feathers all around their body.
  • Human Form- may include traits from their Fera Form (Ex. Bubs’ eyes).
  • Anthro Form- They’re furries. Just furries.
  • Animal Form- They can take form of any non-extinct animals.

Common Behaviors: Animalistic behaviors (though most can be controlled)
Eating Habits: Feratrans can be Omnivores, Carnivores, or Herbivores.
Preferred Habitat: Depends on the Feratran. Some live in forests and live as animals. Most prefer to live among humans.
Mating Rituals: Feratrans have beauty standards just like humans. Hair is liked a big deal for Feratrans, some fluff out their hair to seem more attractive.
Family Structure: Feratrans adopted human values of family. Though basic instincts hold.
Life Stages: Feratran animal forms age base of the equivalent of human to animals years. (EX: Bubs is still a kitten at age 16, she’s not full grown and would be about 8 to 9 months in cat years)
Way of Dress: Feratrans wear human style though, clothes are enchanted so they disappear, grow, or shrink when they change forms.
Religion?: They have “Gods” kinda. They were the first Feratrans to renter society.
Characters belonging to species: Bubs, Monkey, and Rezio.

Zelskiane
oricula faelis
(plural:Zelskis.)

Brief Synopsis: Zelskis are an evolution between a human and a cat, that developed a lethal poison to defend themselves in the lush forests.
Come From: Europe
Language(s) Spoken: Zelski, and the language of the region they’re from.
Key Physical Attributes: Cat ears and tail.
Anatomy: Ability to hide Zelski ears and tail under certain skin. If they grow their fingernails out they grow into a thick hook, similar to a cat’s claw.
Common Behaviors: Infertility is growing at a massive rate. They travel alone if they are not in a religious cult.
Eating Habits: Zelskis are mainly Carnivorous, but do eat certain acidic fruits. The ones in my story force themselves to eat all kinds of foods, in attempt to assimilate to human culture. They don’t use silverware and eat on mats.
Preferred Habitat: Generally forest areas. Though every Zelski is different.
Family Structure: A Zelski’s baby stages are very very quick, which compels many of them to leave their mothers and fathers very early. However, if one chooses to stay, they will be further educated on religion, and will become a preacher at a young age.
Life Stages:

  • Zelski infant years are the quickest: 1-5 are all fit into 2 years.
  • The years after that are their “normal” years. Each Zelski year is 4 human years until the age of twelve.
  • Years 12-17 are the longest years, and seen as the best years of their lives. They are each 10 human years. The rest of their years are 3 each.
  • lifespan a Zelske would hope for is 236 years.

Way of Dress: In their religion, they do not wear revealing clothing (this goes for both sexes). They are encouraged to cover their Zelski ears to be modest. Zelskis are never to wear colored contacts or dye their hair. If a Zelski wants a piercing, they must get it from a preacher. All jewelry worn must be blessed by a preacher first. Many Zelskis can be seen wearing dice necklaces and various clothing containing a star with 6 sides, similar to an asterisk.
Religion?: Yes! Kezism. Some worshipers are normal, though most are in a cult system that worship the god Keznivizdan, a young girl, given a dice cube by her father before his death, and by rolling it she is given the directions to the holy land, and chosen as a divine god, she is always depicted with her Zelski ears covered. Most Kezian rules are shown above in the way of dress section. The most important rule of Kezism is that you must not try to change your fate, you must stay quiet and let god guide you. A few of my characters are trapped in a Kezian cult. Rules are strict and must not be broken. If parents have more than one child, one of the children is chosen to be a preacher and taught religion from a young age. You must not speak the holy language (Zelski) around ANYONE but a Zelski Kezian.
Characters belonging to species: Silkvre and Different.
