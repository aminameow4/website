Feratran
fera commūtāta
(plural:[thing].)

[Insert photo example here]
Brief Synopsis: Feratrans are humans that are cursed. They’re big fluffy buff dudes.
Land in Story: Description
Land of Origin: Description
Language(s) Spoken: Description
Key Physical Attributes: Tall
Anatomy: Feratrans have four different types:

  • Fera Form- includes chest fur/feathers, pubic fur/feathers, thin fur/feathers all around their body.
  • Human Form- may include traits from their Fera Form (Ex. Bubs’ eyes).
  • Anthro Form- They’re furries. Just furries.
  • Animal Form- They can take form of any non-extinct animals.

Common Behaviors: Animalistic behaviors (though most can be controlled)
Eating Habits: Feratrans can be Omnivores, Carnivores, or Herbivores.
Preferred Habitat: Depends on the Feratran. Some live in forests and live as animals. Most prefer to live among humans.
Mating Rituals: Feratrans have beauty standards just like humans. Hair is liked a big deal for Feratrans, some fluff out their hair to seem more attractive.
Family Structure: Feratrans adopted human values of family. Though basic instincts hold.
Life Stages: Feratran animal forms age base of the equivalent of human to animals years. (EX: Bubs is still a kitten at age 16, she’s not full grown and would be about 8 to 9 months in cat years)
Way of Dress: Feratrans wear human style though, clothes are enchanted so they disappear, grow, or shrink when they change forms.
Religion?: They have “Gods” kinda. They were the first Feratrans to renter society.
Characters belonging to species: Bubs, Monkey, and Rezio.

Zelskiane
oricula faelis
(plural:[thing].)

[Insert photo example here]
Brief Synopsis: Zelskes are an evolution between a human and a cat, that developed a lethal poison to defend themselves in the lush forests
Land in Story: United States
Land of Origin: Europe
Language(s) Spoken: Zelske, and the language of the country they’re from
Key Physical Attributes: Cat ears and tail.
Anatomy: ability to hide Zelske ears and tail under certain skin. If they grow their finger nails out they grow into a thick hook, similar to a cat’s claw
Common Behaviors: Infertility is growing at a massive rate. They travel alone if they are not in a religious cult.
Eating Habits: Zelskes are mainly Carnivorous, but do eat certain acidic fruits. The ones in my story force themselves to eat all kinds of foods, in attempt to assimilate to human culture. They don’t use silverware and eat on mats.
Preferred Habitat: Forest areas. Though every Zelske is different.
Mating Rituals: Zelske ears are sensitive and in religion encouraged to cover them. Female Zelskes that have longer hair are seen as more beautiful.
Family Structure: A Zelske’s baby stages are very very quick, which compels many of them to leave their mothers and fathers very early. However, if one chooses to stay, they will be further educated on religion, and will become a preacher at a young age.
Life Stages:

  • baby years are the quickest 1-5 are all fit into 2 years.
  • The years after that are their “normal” years. Each 1 year is 3 human years until the age of twelve.
  • 12 13 14 and 15 are the longest years, and seen as the best years of their lives. They are each 7 human years.
  • The rest of their years are 3 each. The lifespan a Zelske would hope for is 236 years.

Way of Dress: In their religion, they do not wear revealing clothing (this goes for both sexes). They are encouraged to cover their Zelske ears to be modest. Zelskes are never to wear colored contacts or dye their hair. If a Zelske wants a piercing, they must get it from a preacher. All jewelry worn must be blessed by a preacher first. Many Zelskes can be seen wearing dice necklaces and various clothing containing a star with 6 sides, similar to an asterisk.
Religion?: Yes! Kezism. Some worshipers are normal, though most are in a cult system that worship the god Keznivizdan, a young girl, given a dice cube by her father before his death, and by rolling it she is given the directions to the holy land, and chosen as a divine god, she is always depicted with her Zelske ears covered. Most Kezian rules are shown above in the way of dress section. The most important rule of Kezism is that you must not try to change your fate, you must stay quiet and let god guide you. A few of my characters are trapped in a Kezian cult. Rules are strict and must not be broken. If parents have more than one child, one of the children is chosen to be a preacher and taught religion from a young age. You must not speak the holy language (Zelske) around ANYONE but a Zelske Kezian
Characters belonging to species: Silkvre and Different.
