About
 Puck is a strange wanderer from a far off land, or at least that’s what we think. We don’t know much about him other than he showed up one day, and he hasn’t left since. He can be seen either with his traveling theatre, where he either humiliates or annoys someone he lured in to appease some sort of fake crowd that he performs for,or just causing mischief within the many cities. We tried kicking him out, but he either evades the guards or he plays tricks on them. This usually cause one or more of the guards to get hurt, so now we just let him be.
