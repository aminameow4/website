Hi! Felhzverse is a series of drawn and written stories made by a group of kids who really like fiction. We do a little bit of everything, and decided to put that to use by making this site. We've had these characters and concepts for years, and have always wanted to make something to show the world, but never knew what specific format we'd go with(Game? Visual novel? Comic? Aniamted series? No idea). After excessive reading of books and comics, we decided to just settle with a mix of the two here. We hope you like what you see!
