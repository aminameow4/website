Hi! Felhzverse is a series of webcomics and written stories made by a group of kids who really like fiction. We're sort of artists, sort of musicians, sort of writers, and sort of developers(this website was made from scratch). Honestly, we made this website on impulse after seeing the downfall of other multiple-timeline series. We've had these characters and series for years, and have always wanted to make our own published series, but never knew what specific format we'd go with(Game? Visual novel? Comic? Aniamted series? We were clueless.), and decided to start doing comics and writing, because that's what we know a lot about.
