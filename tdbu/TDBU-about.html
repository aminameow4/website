"The Differences Between Us" (written by Shiro) is a story about a teenage kid who gets caught up in the shenanigans of a prince from another universe, who apparently wants to overthrow his parents from the royal throne to restore peace to his planet and the people who inhabit it. If only he could see the difference between a human and some sort of dragon hybrid with magical powers.
