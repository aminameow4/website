Hey here are the characters so far-
Bluzie R.


Pronouns: She/Her; They/Them
Birthday: June 21, 2002
Age: 13
About: Bluzie's an outspoken edgy 13 year old. She has and unexplainable love for fire and all things warm, and enjoys hanging out with friends, drawing, and playing video games. Bluzie is usually the one to lead her friends into all sorts of trouble out of boredom, then try her best to get them out of said trouble. She's known to be honest to a fault, and tries to be as reliable as possible.
Random T.


Pronouns: He/Him
Birthday: June 13, 2002
Age: 13
About: Random is a pretty quiet kid. He doesn't like to stand out much, and prefers to be left to his own devices. Leave him by himself with his laptop and he'll be perfectly fine. He tries to be kind and respectful of everyone around him and their boundaries, although he can at times become frustrated and more difficult to approach. He usually tags along with Bluzie on whatever her decided adventure of the day is, whether he was forced to by his parents, or of his own accord.
Lily T.


Pronouns: She/Her
Birthday: November 16, 2002
Age: 12
About: Lily is indesputably the smartest of the friend group. Although she can be a bit shy around new people, she's a very loving and affectionate friend, and always tries to be around to lend a hand. Don't mistake her soft nature for weakness though, because she is prepared to stand up for herself and others if necessary. She loves flower crowns, reading, dancing, and all things considered 'cute'.
Clover S.


Pronouns: She/Her;
Birthday: November 21, 2002
Age: 12
About: Enthusiastic, athletic, and outgoing, Clover is the social butterfly of the group. She's just here to do her very best in school, and have everything she believes defines a teenager. She might sometimes become distracted by a crush, but she always tries to stay on track with any goal. Although she sometimes comes off as shallow, Clover really does mean well.
