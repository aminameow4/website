Characters

Silkvre Mistero
Pronouns: She/Her
Birthday: 7/4/1969
Age: 9 and 3/4 Zelski years
About: She’s a fearless leader type character. She can be seen as manipulative. She acts before she thinks and she’s very loud about everything. She takes many serious things as a joke

Different LeBeau
Pronouns: He/Him
Age: 10 Zelski years
About: French immigrant who went blind at a young age due to an attack in the forest he lives in. He is more a follower than a leader, and feels happiness is just as important as power. He wants to enjoy the world around him and has no malicious intent in his rebellion at first.

Gold Mistero
Pronouns: He/Him
Birthday: 1/8/1958
Age: 12 Zelski years
About: Gold is the highly religious brother of Silkvre Mistero. He’s intelligent and clever, but can be depressed, and has mood swings on occasion.
